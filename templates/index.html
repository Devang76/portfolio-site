About Me

I’m Devang Mestry, an Artificial Intelligence and Data Science graduate based in Mumbai, driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and a strong belief in data-driven decision making. My journey into data science was shaped as much by the football field as by the classroom - sports taught me discipline, strategy, and the power of analysis, lessons that naturally translated into how I approach technology and data today.

Academically trained with a strong foundation in AI, machine learning, data analytics, and optimization, I enjoy building practical solutions that blend intelligence with usability. From developing AI-powered applications like Dietify, a cross-platform diet consultation system integrating LLMs and real-world APIs, to working on deep learning models for industrial crack detection, my work focuses on solving real problems with measurable impact. My experiences span analytics, full-stack development, IoT, and automation, reinforced through multiple internships and research-driven projects.

Beyond technical work, I’ve taken on leadership roles - serving as CEO of the Society for Data Science, managing large student teams, organizing national-level hackathons, and currently working as a Technology Educator and District Coordinator, where I simplify complex technologies like AI, IoT, and robotics for wider adoption. Whether I’m analyzing datasets, leading teams, teaching technology, or pushing my limits through sports and trekking, I’m motivated by continuous learning and creating meaningful, real-world impact through technology.
